about us

Located in the heart of Beirut, and engaged with global network of agencies worldwide, MSL is offering its services in the field of shipping & freight forwarding business.

Cost-effective solutions, trust-worthy consultancy, secure shipping & fast delivery are our main targets as MSL.

Shipping cargo is not a complex matter from now on. MSL will undertake the mission of shipping customer’s cargo from A to Z: starting from collecting cargo, to freight & even customs clearance. MSL ensures fast & safe shipment delivery all around the world at economic and competitive shipping rates.

OUR MISSION

Your Shipping Needs. Our Solutions. Our mission at MSL is to grant solutions for all customers’ shipping needs & expertly coordinates the transportation of goods using multi-modal modes of transport to ensure on time delivery.

OUR VISION

More than Shipping. At MSL, our vision is to make shipping as flexible as possible so that the customer will enjoy the experience of moving cargo.

EXPANDING GOAL

Your Vital Link to World Trade. The main target at MSL is to be one of the world’s leaders in shipping & logistics services, and to make positive change in worldwide market by utilizing new technologies & developing our own strategies.
